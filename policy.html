Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

SUTD Timetable Sync (“the extension”) helps students import their SUTD class timetable directly into Google Calendar. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how your data is handled.

1. Information We Access

The extension may access the following information only when you choose to sync:

  • Your Google account email (read-only)
  • Permission to create events in your Google Calendar
  • Your timetable data from the SUTD portal (class names, dates, times, and locations)

2. What We Do Not Do

The extension does not:

  • Collect or store your data on external servers
  • Share or sell any information to third parties
  • Track your behavior or usage
  • Read, modify, or delete existing Google Calendar events

3. Data Storage

All timetable data is processed locally on your device. No data is stored, transmitted, or retained by the developer.

4. Google OAuth Permissions

The extension uses Google OAuth solely for user-initiated syncing of timetable events. You may revoke access at any time from your Google Account:
https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the extension indicates acceptance of any changes.
